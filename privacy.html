Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026em>

1. Who we are

8Impact is a privacy‑first fundraising tool that allows supporters to complete surveys to generate money for creators. The platform is designed to operate with minimal data collection.

2. What we do not collect

8Impact does not ask for or store:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Passwords or accounts
  • Payment card details
  • Demographic information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers

3. Anonymous creator IDs

Creators use anonymous IDs (example: CREATOR_ABC123) to receive earnings. These IDs contain no personal information and are generated locally.

4. Payout passphrases

Each creator receives a private payout passphrase. This passphrase is never stored by 8Impact and is required to claim earnings.

5. Surveys and third‑party providers

Surveys are provided by CPX Research. When interacting with surveys, users are interacting directly with CPX Research, which may collect limited information needed to operate surveys and prevent fraud.

6. Cookies and tracking

8Impact does not set cookies or use tracking pixels. CPX Research may use cookies inside their survey iframe.

7. Payouts

Payouts are tracked using anonymous creator IDs and payout amounts. No personal payment details are stored on this site.

8. Data security

Because 8Impact intentionally avoids collecting personal data, the risk of data exposure is significantly reduced.

9. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the 8Impact maintainer via the GitHub repository.
